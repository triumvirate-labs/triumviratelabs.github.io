## Mobile App Privacy Policy Example ##
Last Updated: November 26, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Triumvirate Labs LLC handles information for users of out mobile applications.

1. Information We Do Not Collect
We want to be perfectly clear: The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about its users.

This means we do not collect:

Personal Identifiable Information (PII): We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, IP addresses, or unique device identifiers.

User Activity or Usage Data: We do not track what you do within the app, how often you use it, or which features you interact with.

Third-Party Data: We do not share any data with third-party advertisers or analytics providers because we do not have any data to share.

In short, your use of the App is private and anonymous to us.

2. In-App Purchases (IAPs)
The App offers in-app purchases to unlock premium content or features.

Handling of Payment Information: All purchases are processed directly by the respective platform provider (e.g., Apple App Store or Google Play Store) using their secure payment systems. We do not receive, store, or have access to your payment information, credit card details, or billing address.

Purchase Validation: To deliver the content or features you purchased, the App communicates with the platform provider (Apple or Google) to verify the transaction. This communication is handled securely by the operating system and is only used to confirm the status of your purchase and restore it if needed.

3. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the App or on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
